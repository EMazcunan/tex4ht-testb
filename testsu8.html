3.8   Command definition

The Airy function, Ai ⁡ ( x ) , may be incorrectly defined as this integral

Ai ⁡ ( x ) = ∫ exp ⁡ ( s 3 + isx ) ds .

This vector identity serves nicely to illustrate two of the new commands:

∇ ⁡ × q = i ∂w ∂y − ∂v ∂z + j ∂u ∂z − ∂w ∂x + k ∂v ∂x − ∂u ∂y .
